LUSAIL STADIUM OPERATIONAL GUIDE
===============================
Stadium capacity: 80,000.
Gates and Entrances:
- Gate A (North): Primary entrance for general admission. Connects directly to the Lusail Metro Station Shuttle Bus lines.
- Gate B (East): Dedicated entrance for VIP, hospitality ticket holders, and official FIFA delegates. Has direct access to Parking Lot Alpha.
- Gate C (South): General admission. Closest entrance to the central Taxi/Rideshare drops.
- Gate D (West): Media, Broadcast crews, and wheelchair-accessible entry. Connects to accessible lift routes for level 1 and level 2 seating.

Emergency Protocols:
- Primary Assembly Point: Sector North (Green Lawn, outside Gate A).
- Secondary Assembly Point: Sector West (Parking Beta, outside Gate D).
- Evacuation pathways: Sector A exits through Gate A. Sector B exits through Gate B. Sector C exits through Gate C. Sector D exits through Gate D.

Accessibility:
- Wheelchair ramps are fully operational at Gate D.
- Audio guide loops are configured for Level 1, blocks 101 to 110.
- Lifts are located at elevator banks E1 and E2 near Sector West.

Sustainability:
- Lusail stadium targets zero-waste-to-landfill. High-grade food composters are located at food courts FC-1 and FC-2.
- Smart LED lighting schedules are automatically regulated by the operations dashboard.
